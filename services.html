My services

Sworn translation

Sworn translators in Luxembourg feature on a list published by the Ministry of Justice. They are entitled to certify accurate translations of official documents sealed with their stamp and signature.
Such translations can be produced to authorities in Luxembourg and abroad.

The translation consists in reporting various types of official documents in a foreign language, including for example procedural documents, notary deeds, administrative documents (marriage records, divorce deeds, birth certificates, diplomas, etc.).

Business and
conference interpreting

There are three main types of interpreting

Liaison

For trade fairs, company audits and business meetings, it involves an interpreter working from and into languages A and B, acting as an intermediary between the parties.

Consecutive

This mode used in congresses and presentations involves note-taking of 5-10 min speeches in language B that are then rendered in language A right after the speaker is done.

Simultaneous

best-known in the EU and the UN, the booth offers seamless communication in real time across the language barrier. The interpreter listen to language B and at the same time recreates the message in language A in a microphone, as the audience listens through earphones.
